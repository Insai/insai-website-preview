The Challenge

Sleep-EEG analysis is hard to reproduce

Staging and biomarker pipelines are often hand-built per study: tuned to one dataset, one montage, one scorer. The numbers rarely transfer, and the methods are hard for the next lab to reproduce.

The result: findings that don't replicate, and months spent rebuilding analysis that should already exist.

The missing piece isn't more data. It's reproducible measurement.

How It Works

From raw recordings to reproducible results

Sleep-EEG carries rich neurophysiological signal that can be captured at home over many nights. Insai turns that signal into structured, validated biomarkers your lab can trust and reproduce.

Capture
Collect multi-night EEG at home with research wearables, or analyze existing PSG and EEG from your archive. Real-world data across days and weeks, not single-visit snapshots.
Analyze
Published staging models and biomarker methods, each reproduced to a 1e-3 tolerance on public cohorts, turn raw EEG into sleep architecture, spectral measures, and event markers with confidence scores.
Report
Structured biomarker outputs delivered as data tables, visual reports, or via API, each with its confidence scores and validation record. Built for analysis, sharing, and publication.
Validated results from real-world sleep data. Methods another lab can reproduce. Less time rebuilding pipelines, more time on the science.
