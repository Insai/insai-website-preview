FAQ

Technical FAQ

How do you handle multi-site EEG variability? #
Our models are validated across 6+ independent cohorts with different recording equipment, protocols, and demographics. Cross-site generalization is a design principle, not an afterthought.
What is your approach to regulatory qualification? #
Insai is designed for regulatory workflows. Our outputs include audit trails, version-controlled model documentation, and qualification-ready evidence packages. Our platform is for research use only.
What data formats do you accept? #
EDF, EDF+, BDF, and proprietary PSG formats from major clinical systems. We also support raw binary formats with channel mapping specifications.
What is the typical turnaround time? #
Batch processing of a typical Phase 2 study (200-500 recordings) completes within 24-48 hours. Single-recording analysis returns results in under 5 minutes.
How does Insai handle data security? #
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We process data in isolated environments. No patient-identifiable information is required for biomarker extraction.
Can Insai work with wearable EEG devices? #
Yes. Our models are designed for cross-device generalization. We support clinical PSG and headband EEG today, with in-ear EEG on the roadmap.
What biomarkers can Insai extract? #
Sleep architecture metrics, spectral power features, sleep spindle characteristics, slow-wave activity, and composite brain health scores. Custom biomarker development is available for specific clinical endpoints.
How do you validate against gold-standard scoring? #
Our models are validated against expert polysomnography scoring from board-certified sleep technologists. We report concordance metrics, sensitivity, specificity, and Cohen's kappa for each biomarker.
